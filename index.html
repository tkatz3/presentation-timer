Presentation Timer

Countdown & Stopwatch

Use the top toggle to switch between Countdown and Stopwatch modes. Set your time using the arrow buttons or click the numbers to type directly. Recently used timers appear as quick-start chips.

Keep Screen On

Prevents your display from sleeping while you're on this page — enabled by default (green dot = active). Important: this only works while you're on this tab. If you switch to another tab or app, the screen can still sleep. To keep your screen on while multitasking, make sure the floating mini-player is open (see below).

Floating Mini-Player

Opens a small floating window showing your timer that stays on top of other apps. By default, it auto-opens when you switch away from this tab and closes when you come back. You can also open it manually from the bottom bar. While the mini-player is visible, your screen will stay on even if you're in another app.

Overtime

When a countdown reaches zero, the screen flashes and the timer automatically switches to counting overtime (shown in red with a + prefix). Pause at any time to adjust.

Sound Alerts

Choose from three options in Settings: Off, Chime (a gentle tone plays once when time is up), or Alarm (the same chime repeats every few seconds until you dismiss it by clicking the red banner).

Keyboard Shortcuts

Space Start / Pause   R Reset   Esc Exit fullscreen

Offline

This timer works entirely in your browser with no server required. All features (timing, sound, wake lock, PiP) use local browser APIs and will continue working if your internet disconnects.
